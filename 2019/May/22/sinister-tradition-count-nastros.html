Album Review: Sinister Tradition - Count Nastros

May 22, 2019

Hateful and Satanic Raw Black Metal at it's finest. That's the best way to describe Sinister Tradition, a Raw Black Metal project from Colorado, created by Nastros. Sinister Tradition's "Count Nastros" is a very raw full-lenght album which consists of 6 tracks of sinister ambiental noises and Raw Satanic Black Metal. Drums are remarcably high quality, but the guitars are raw, cold and hateful. Vocals are very harsh and shreaking while conjuring and manipulating true satanism, and necromancy into the music's lyrical themes. Last but not least, the 6th track is featuring Swamp Temple. All in all, this is some of the best Satanic Black Metal I've heard made in our days. Listen and Buy here.
