New release: Zimorog - The Theory of Theory

May 21, 2019

Melancholic and cold riffs, lo-fi drums, raspy vocals. Those are the specifics of Zimorog, a one-man Raw Black Metal band from Slovakia founded in 2014, which covers lyrical themes such as winter, nature, people and more. Zimorog's latest release, "The Theory of Theory", is a very raw and primitive form of Black Metal. It consists of 9 tracks, all filled with lo-fi drums, melancholic/misanthropic guitar riffs and very raspy mid-low vocals. The production is very cold and raw. Definetly a release you should check out. Listen and Buy here.
